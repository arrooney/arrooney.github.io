Climbing

I have been climbing since the age of 8, and ever since beginning I haven't been able to stop!

For years I have trained for competitions on three different teams in the city, now I spend time on the weekends coaching young climbers.

Competition climbing has taken me all around the province, attending numerous provincial competitions. I have also traveled to Vancouver and Toronto to take part in the youth national championships.



I've also spent many wonderful weekends in the Canmore climbing on real rock, and in 2016 embarked on an adventure to Squamish, BC to do some boulder climbing (pictured here).

In 2017 I packed up my rope and went climbing all around New Zealand, for which I owe much gratitude to all the helpful Kiwi's who let me hitch a ride with them around the countryside.
